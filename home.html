Services

Amazing Features

Membership Management

Elevate Your Association with Intelligent CRM. Manage membership data with ease. Profiles are flexible to accommodate your needs with custom fields, groups or segment records by: member, non-member, member type, committee type, and more.

Event Management

Elevate Your Events. A small meeting, major convention, or virtual conference; our event management tools allow you to offer online registration, payment, collecting information such as session selections, meal options and additional information.

Communication

Connect Effortlessly, Communicate Seamlessly. Email marketing, scheduling tools, list segmentation and automation capabilities, create event registration emails that allow members into your upcoming events.

Mobile Web App

Connect, Create, Conquer - All from Your Mobile. Quick and easy access to information on events, member directory, and all documents housed on the site.

Reporting

Reporting Redefined: Powerful, Precise, Proactive. Single event registration, cumulative attendance, site access reports, email tracking, and much more.

Onboarding/Support

Onboarding Made Effortless. A dedicated support team guides every new account through a custom onboarding process to ensure we are fully optimized for your organization. Our customer support team is available by phone, email, or online chat.
